Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?
We offer full-stack web development, UI/UX design, branding, and graphic design including logos, flyers, posters, and social media content.
Do you only work with websites?
No — in addition to websites and web apps, we also provide custom graphic design solutions for businesses, events, and personal brands.
Who are the people behind the brand?
The brand is co-founded by Oladipupo Christmike (Creative Lead) and Caleb Abraham (Technical Lead). Together, they deliver bold, elegant, and high-performing digital work.
How long does a project usually take?
It depends on the project size. A typical website can take 2–4 weeks. Graphic design projects can be delivered in as little as 1–5 days depending on complexity.
Do you provide revisions?
Absolutely! We include revision rounds in all our packages to make sure you’re 100% satisfied with the final result.
How can I get started?
Reach out to us here. We’ll schedule a quick consultation to understand your needs and kick off the project.
